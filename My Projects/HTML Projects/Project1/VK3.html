COVID - 19

FAQs :

1. What is coronavirus and COVID - 19?

  • Coronavirus is a virus which affects the respiratory system of animals including humans.
    It is of diameter approximately equal to 0.1 micrometer.

  • COVID - 19 is disease caused by coronavirus in an animal body including humans.

2. How does this virus spread and how does this disease affect people?

  • Coronavirus generally spread through direct contact but recent researches show that it can spread through air as well.

  • Symptoms for COVID - 19 includes :

    • fever,
    • cough,
    • tiredness,
    • loss of taste and smell,

    and other rare symptoms including sore throat, red eyes, etc.

3. What precautions should be taken to minimize our chances for infection against this virus?

    Precautions to be taken to minimize the infection :-

  • Wear a mask.
  • Maintain social distancing.
  • Frequently cleanse your body, especially hands.
